### How to Unlock Characters

Characters in Ultimate Smashbox can be unlocked through various methods:

1. **Story Mode Progression**: Complete specific story chapters
2. **Arcade Mode**: Beat arcade mode with different characters
3. **Special Conditions**: Meet hidden requirements during battles
4. **Time Attack**: Complete time attack under certain time limits
5. **Versus Battles**: Win a specific number of versus matches

### Character Select

Unlocked characters will automatically appear in the character select screen under their respective categories:
- UM/CC - Capcom Characters
- UM/AH - Anime Heroes
- UM/Anime - Anime Fighters
- UM/BB - BlazBlue Characters
